The article discusses how security teams can leverage IP intelligence data to improve decision-making, moving from reactive to proactive operations by combining geolocation, network ownership, privacy signals, and internal telemetry. The author highlights three recurring challenges analysts face: prioritizing alerts deserving immediate attention, obtaining intelligence earlier in workflows, and ensuring data accuracy reflects current internet conditions. Combining multiple types of IP context creates stronger operational security outcomes than relying on single data sources.
